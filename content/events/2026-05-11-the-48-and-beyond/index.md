We're teaming up with [San Diego Media Pros](https://sdmediapros.org) for their
May meeting, an evening workshop focused on what it really takes to succeed in
the 48 Hour Film Project and how those same skills apply on professional and
indie productions.

Join **Chrissy Raven Kilna** and **Kilna, Anthony** as they deconstruct the
"48" experience, moving beyond the ticking clock to show how to build a
high-functioning creative team and thrive under deadline pressure.

## What You'll Learn

- The blueprint for participation, from rules and kickoff to the final drop-off.
- How to build a reliable team of cast and crew for a 48-hour production sprint.
- Workflow strategies for managing the weekend from script to final render.
- How 48 Hour Film Project experience translates to commercial and indie sets.

## Presenters

- **Chrissy Raven Kilna**, City Producer and community coordinator for the San Diego 48 Hour Film Project
- **Kilna, Anthony**, City Producer and lead technologist for the San Diego 48 Hour Film Project
