Congratulations! You've made it through the 48 Hour Film Project competition weekend. Now it's time to celebrate your accomplishment and share your filmmaking journey with fellow 48ers.

## What to expect

- **Celebration**: Toast to your team's hard work and creativity
- **War Stories**: Share the highs, lows, and hilarious moments from your 48-hour adventure
- **Networking**: Connect with other filmmakers and potential future collaborators
- **Community**: Be part of the San Diego filmmaking community

## Who should attend

- **All team members** who participated in the competition
- **Supporters** and friends of the 48 Hour Film Project
- **Anyone interested** in hearing about the filmmaking process
- **Future participants** who want to learn what to expect

## Location details

The wrap party location will be announced closer to the event date. We're working on securing a great venue that can accommodate all our filmmakers and supporters.

## What to bring

- Your team's stories and experiences
- Photos or behind-the-scenes footage from your shoot
- Excitement about seeing all the films at the premiere (date TBD)
- A sense of accomplishment - you just made a film in 48 hours!

## After the wrap party

The journey isn't over yet! Keep an eye out for:
- **Film premiere** details (typically held a few weeks after the competition)
- **Awards ceremony** information
- **Next year's competition** registration

You've accomplished something amazing - making a complete film in just 48 hours. Celebrate that achievement and enjoy the community you've become part of!
